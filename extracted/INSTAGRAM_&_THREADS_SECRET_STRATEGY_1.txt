=== PAGE 14 ===
GEO TARGETING & DISTRIBUTION CONTROL
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
When to Use Geo Blocking
Geo blocking should only be activated if multiple of the
following are true:
Reels are consistently pushed to the wrong
countries
Engagement is high but monetization is low
Audience is dominated by non-English comments
Repeated USA signaling (music, captions,
locations) has failed
Account has been running for several weeks
already
⚠ Do not use geo blocking on brand-new accounts.
Approved Method (Instagram Native Only)
Instagram does not allow direct country blocking.
Instead, we use Age Restriction by Country to suppress
visibility.
Step-by-Step Setup
1.Go to Instagram Settings
2.Select Business / Creator Tools
3.Open Minimum Age
4.Choose Add Country
5.Select the country you want to limit
6.Set the highest minimum age Instagram allows
7.Repeat for additional countries if needed
This reduces visibility in those regions without triggering hard
flags.

=== PAGE 18 ===
CONCLUSION
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
This SOP is not a fixed playbook. It is a framework for
controlled experimentation. The exact posts, angles, and
narratives that work will change over time, but the
underlying logic does not.
The system succeeds because it separates signal creation
from distribution. Threads is used to manufacture
engagement, validate identity, and warm traffic without
risk. 
Instagram is used only once the account already appears
wanted. This order is not optional. Reversing it leads to
suppression, wasted content, or account loss.
Growth should never be forced. If engagement slows, the
correct response is not to post more, but to observe, adjust,
and iterate. 
Small changes to framing, narrative, or presentation are often
enough to restart momentum. Large changes, such as
switching models entirely, are unnecessary and harmful.
This strategy rewards patience and consistency.
Accounts that follow the warmup rules, respect posting
limits, and respond intelligently to engagement will
compound over time. 
Accounts that rush visibility, chase trends blindly, or
ignore feedback will stall regardless of content quality.
Treat every post as data. Track what earns comments,
profile visits, and followers. Repeat what works,
abandon what does not, and allow the system time to
stabilize before making decisions.
This SOP does not guarantee instant results. It
guarantees direction. Operators who execute with
discipline, adapt intelligently, and stay consistent will
eventually reach scale.
Patience beats aggression.
