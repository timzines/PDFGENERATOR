=== PAGE 3 ===
INTRO: WHY HIRING MATTERS
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
Chatters are the human layer of the system. They control communication, emotions, and
revenue. A bad hire does not just reduce income. A bad hire can damage accounts, leak
methods, or sabotage operations.
Hiring is not about finding cheap labor. It is about finding reliable operators who can follow
structure and respect boundaries.
You are not hiring personalities. You are hiring discipline.
Every chatter must be filtered. Every chatter must be tested. Every chatter must be proven
before trust is given.
This guide exists to reduce risk and remove guessing from the hiring process.

=== PAGE 5 ===
FIRST SCREENING
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
Before any interview, applicants must be screened.
You should check:
 Previous work history.
 Basic English level from their written messages.
 Their communication tone and professionalism.
If a candidate cannot write clearly, politely, and in full sentences, they should not
move forward.
This stage is about eliminating obvious bad fits before wasting time on interviews.


=== PAGE 6 ===
INTERVIEW PROCESS
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
Every candidate must go through a live interview.
The interview should cover:
 Previous chatting or sales work
 Experience with adult platforms if any
 Availability and time zone
 Motivation for the job
The interview is not just about answers. It is about
behavior.
You must observe:
 How they speak
 How confident they are in English
 How they respond under pressure
 How honest they seem about past work
Candidates who avoid direct answers or exaggerate
experience should be rejected.

=== PAGE 7 ===
ENGLISH AND SKILL TESTING
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
After the interview, every candidate must be tested
live.
They must join a Discord call with screen sharing.
English test:
 They must answer questions verbally.
 They must write sample replies in chat.
 This prevents cheating with translators or AI
tools.
Typing speed test:
 A words per minute test must be done live.
 This shows how fast they can respond to fans.
If a candidate refuses screen sharing or avoids live testing, they
should be rejected immediately.

=== PAGE 8 ===
OBSERVATION PERIOD
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsNo candidate should get direct access after testing.
They must first observe trained chatters for one to two days.
During this period, they should:
 Watch how real conversations look
 Understand tone and structure
 Learn pacing and discipline
This stage filters out people who lose interest quickly or cannot focus.
Only candidates who stay attentive and engaged should move forward.

=== PAGE 9 ===
ACCESS CONTROL
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsAccess must be earned gradually.
New chatters should never receive full account access immediately.
First stage:
 Read only access
 Observation only
Second stage:
 Limited supervised chatting
 No financial controls
Final stage:
 Full chatting access after trust is built
This reduces damage if a hire turns out to be malicious or incompetent

=== PAGE 10 ===
PAYMENT STRUCTURE
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsStandard pay should be structured as follows:
Hourly pay between 5 and 7 dollars per hour
 Commission of around 3 percent of profits
This creates stability through hourly pay and
motivation through commission.
Payment schedule:
 Biweekly payments
Payment method:
 Wise is preferredClear payment rules must be set before work begins.
Never delay payments.
 Never change pay terms without agreement.
A chatter who is paid on time is more loyal.
 A chatter who is not paid becomes a risk.

PERFORMANCE TRACKING
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsChatters must track their own performance.
They must use Excel to log:
 Hours worked
 Sales made
 Messages sent if required
You must regularly verify these stats to ensure they are
accurate.
Random checks should be done to confirm:
 Chat activity
 Revenue numbers
 Time claims
Self reporting saves management time but requires
verification to prevent manipulation.

=== PAGE 12 ===
RISK AND TRUST MANAGEMENT
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsChatters can cause serious damage if mistreated.
They can leak methods.
 They can sabotage accounts.
 They can spread negative reputation.
For this reason:
 Always treat chatters with respect
 Always pay them on time
 Always communicate clearlyFear based management creates short term
compliance but long term risk.
Trust is built through fairness, consistency, and
transparency.
A well treated chatter protects your system.
 A mistreated chatter becomes a threat.
