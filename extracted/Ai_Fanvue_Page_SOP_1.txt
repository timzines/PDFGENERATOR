an advertisement.

BIO: IDENTITY AND EMOTIONAL HOOK
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
The bio should sound like a person, not a business. It must
feel emotional rather than technical.
Example style:
 Just a girl who loves late nights and private talks. I share my
daily life and my naughty side here. Stay and get to know me
better.The bio should answer three questions:
 Who are you.
 What happens here.
 Why should I stay.
A good bio structure is simple.
First line explains personality or mood.
Second line explains what type of content or interaction
happens here.
Third line invites the fan emotionally.
A bad bio focuses on features instead of feelings.
Daily posts. HD videos. Cheap PPV.
Features do not create desire. Feelings create desire.
The bio should never look like a shop description. It should look
like a short personal introduction.

=== PAGE 16 ===
Subscriber Pricing
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsThe subscription price is not designed to generate profit by
itself. Its main purpose is to remove fear and make the fan
take the first step.
The real money is not made from subscriptions. The real
money is made from chatting and PPV. The subscription exists
to unlock communication and emotional connection, not to be
the main revenue source.
The base subscription price should be positioned between 10
and 20 dollars. This creates perceived value and prevents the
page from looking cheap or low quality.
A promotion discount should always be applied on Fanvue to
lower the visible price to around 3 to 9 dollars. This makes the
decision easy and removes psychological resistance.
The fan should feel that he is getting access at a
reduced price and that joining is a low risk decision.
The objective of the subscription is not income.
 The objective is conversion.
Once the fan subscribes, he can comment, reply, and
engage. This allows emotional attachment to be built
through chatting. Emotional attachment is what later
creates spending through PPV and private interactions.
Income from subscriptions is a small bonus.
Income from chatting and PPV is the real business
model.

CONTENT PROGRESSION MODEL
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsContent must follow a clear ladder.
Free content builds curiosity.
 Subscriber content builds desire.
 PPV content fulfills fantasy.
If a fan can get satisfaction for free, he will not
subscribe.
If a fan can get full satisfaction from subscription,
he will not buy PPV.Each layer must leave something unfinished so the
next layer becomes attractive.
Example progression:
 Free outfit photo
 Subscriber lingerie photo
 PPV nude video
This progression is what turns attention into
revenue.

=== PAGE 18 ===
VISUAL CONSISTENCY
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
All content must look like it comes from the same person.
Backgrounds should be similar.
 Camera angles should be similar.
 Style and mood should be similar.
Hair, makeup, and clothing style should not change drastically. Sudden changes reduce realism
and trust.
Consistency creates realism. Realism creates attachment. Attachment creates spending.

Summary
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsThis document defines how a Fanvue page must be structured to function as a conversion system rather than a
content dump. Every part of the page exists to guide a fan emotionally from curiosity to trust and from trust to
spending.
The banner establishes the fantasy and emotional mood. The bio creates identity and invites connection. Free
posts build realism and safety. Subscriber posts introduce intimacy and exclusivity. PPV content fulfills the
fantasy and delivers the highest value. Automated messages begin and reinforce emotional attachment through
conversation.
Pricing and content progression are designed to remove fear, create perceived value, and increase volume.
Visual consistency protects realism and prevents emotional breakage. Each layer leaves something unfinished so
the next layer becomes attractive.
A successful Fanvue page does not feel like a store. It feels like a private world. The fan is not pushed to buy. He
is guided to stay. Emotional connection is created first. Revenue follows naturally.
This SOP exists to replace randomness with structure and to turn a Fanvue profile into a predictable revenue
environment.
