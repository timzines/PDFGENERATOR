=== PAGE 3 ===
INTRO: THE ROLE OF TIKTOK
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
TikTok is a secondary traffic source.
 Instagram remains the main platform.
TikTok exists to:
 Recycle content
 Generate extra views
 Capture additional attention
TikTok converts worse than Instagram.
 Estimated 5 to 10 times worse in CVR.
Its value is not volume.
 
Its value is extra reach when combined with other
organic marketing.
TikTok should support the system.
 Not replace it.

=== PAGE 4 ===
PLATFORM RISK: TIKTOK VS INSTAGRAM
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
TikTok is stricter than Instagram.
Content moderation is heavier.
 Accounts are reviewed faster.
 Shadow limits happen more easily.
Because of this:
 Only very safe content should be posted.
TikTok is not the place to push boundaries.
 
What works on Instagram can get limited on TikTok.If content feels risky:
 Do not post it on TikTok.
TikTok rewards accounts that:
 Look human
 Act slowly
 Stay within platform comfort zones

=== PAGE 5 ===
ACCOUNT SETUP: FIRST SIGNALS
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
New TikTok accounts must start empty.
When creating a new account:
 Do not add username
 Do not add bio
 Do not add profile picture
Close the app for 20 minutes after creation.
Early behavior determines trust.

=== PAGE 7 ===
WARM UP PHASE: NEW ACCOUNT PROTOCOL
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
Warm up is mandatory on TikTok.
Day 1
 Create the account
 No username, bio, or picture
 Close app for 20 minutes
 Browse FYP 30–60 minutes
 No likes
 No follows
 No posts
Day 2
 Browse FYP 30 minutes
 No likes
 No follows
 No posts
Day 3
 Browse FYP 30 minutes
 Like 3–5 videos
 No follows
 No posts
Day 4
 Browse FYP 30–40 minutes
 Like 5–7 videos
 Write 1 comment
 No follows
 No posts
Day 5
 Add profile picture, bio, and username
manually
 Browse FYP 30–40 minutes
 Like 8–10 videos
 Write 2 comments
 Post the first TikTok
Day 6
 Browse FYP 30–40 minutes
 Like 8–10 videos
 Write 2 comments
 No posting
Day 7
 Browse FYP 30–40 minutes
 Like 8–10 videos
 Write 2–3 comments
 Post the second TikTok
Day 8
 Browse FYP 30–40 minutes
 Like 10 videos
 Write 3 comments
 No posting
Day 9
 Browse FYP 30–40 minutes
 Like 10 videos
 Write 3 comments
 Post the third TikTok
After first post:
 Activate analytics
 Profile →  Menu →  Creator
Tools →  Analytics →  Get Started
After Day 9, treat the account
like a regular one.

=== PAGE 8 ===
POSTING PHASE: AFTER WARM UP
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
Posting can increase slowly.
Rules:
 Do not spam ( wait 4+ hours between posts )
 Do not batch upload
 Do not post risky content
Safe posting beats frequent posting.
TikTok punishes speed.
 TikTok rewards stability.

=== PAGE 9 ===
DAILY BEHAVIOR: TRUST SIGNALS
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
Daily actions per account:
 Browse FYP at least 20 minutes
 Like content
 Comment naturally
Follow back 5–10 followers per day.
Before posting:
 Engage 10 minutes
After posting:
 Engage 10 minutes
 Close app for 30 minutes
Captions must be typed manually.
 Never copy-paste captions.

=== PAGE 10 ===
MULTI-ACCOUNT SAFETY
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
Maximum 3 accounts per device.
Each account must have:
 Unique username
 Unique profile picture
 Unique bio
Never open the same link on multiple accounts.
Linked accounts increase ban risk.

=== PAGE 11 ===
CONTENT FORMATS: SAFE REACH & CAROUSELS
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
TikTok does not require only videos.
Carousels are a powerful and safe format.
Carousel examples:
 Pictures of your model
 Your model with celebrities
 Your model in different outfits
 Your model in different moods
Carousels:
 Are easy to consume
 Lower ban risk
 Often get strong reach
Carousels are recommended for:
 New accounts
 Risk-averse posting
 Consistent exposure
