INTRO: THE ROLE OF X
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
X is not a sales page. It is a discovery environment. Its
purpose is to turn strangers into profile visitors and profile
visitors into fans.
A user decides very quickly whether an account feels real or
fake, safe or risky, interesting or boring. If the account looks
spammy, aggressive, or sexual too fast, the user scrolls
away.
The X account must communicate three things immediately:
 Personality
 Authenticity
 Emotional tone
The goal is not to sell. The goal is to be seen.
X is the public stage. Fanvue is the private room.
A good X account feels like a real person inside public conversations, not
like an advertisement.
 The fan should feel curious, not pressured.

BIO: IDENTITY AND EMOTIONAL HOOK
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
The bio should sound like a person, not a business. It must
feel emotional rather than technical.
The bio should answer three questions:
 Who are you
 What happens here
 Why should I follow
A good bio structure is simple.
 First line explains mood or personality.
 Second line explains what type of content appears.
 Third line invites emotionally.
Example style:
 Late nights and quiet trouble. I share little moments and
things I like here. Stay with me.
A bad bio focuses on features instead of feelings.
 Daily posts. HD photos. PPV.
Features do not create desire. Personality creates desire.
The bio should never look like a shop description. It should
look like a short introduction.
BAD EXAMPLE:
 NSFW model. Link below
GOOD EXAMPLE:
 Just a girl who likes long nights and attention.

=== PAGE 9 ===
REPLIES: REACH AND RELATIONSHIP
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
Replies are the main growth engine on X.
Most followers come from being visible under large
posts, not from your own tweets.
Replies should be:
 Short
 Casual
 Relevant
Reply rules.
 Start lowercase.
 Under 15 words.
 One emoji max.
 No links.
 No insults.
 No copy paste.
Good reply style:
 nah that’s wild
 i would’ve done the same
 this feels illegal
Bad reply style:
🔥🔥🔥
 check my bio
 buy my content
Replies must feel like conversation, not promotion.

TARGETING: AUDIENCE AND POOLS
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rights
The goal is not mass attention. The goal is correct
attention.
The main audience is men with spending power,
usually age 40 and above.
They are interested in:
 Business
 Luxury
 Cars
 Travel
 Women
 Comfort lifestyle
They do not use hashtags.
They read replies under big pages.Reply pools:
 Pool A: viral posts
 Pool B: niche creators
 Pool C: small active posts
The account should reply under posts where these
men already read.
Over time, X builds a personality file for the account
and shows it to similar users.

=== PAGE 11 ===
WARM UP: TRUST AND REPUTATION
All materials are strictly protected under AI Influencer Accelerator® rightsNew accounts have low trust. Moving too fast causes
shadowbans and low reach.
Warm up is mandatory.
Days 1 to 3:
 1 text post per day
 10 to 20 likes
 5 replies
 No adult content
Days 4 to 7:
 1 to 2 SFW posts
 30 likes
 10 replies
Days 8 to 14:
 2 to 3 posts
 20 replies
 One short thread
If the account does not pass the trust test, warm up must
continue without posting.
Account health must be checked using this tool:
https://shadowban.yuzurisa.com
Warm up without posting continues until all indicators are
green.
 Only when everything is green is the account ready for real
posting and scaling.
